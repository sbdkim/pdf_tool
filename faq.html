Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files uploaded anywhere?

No. Launch v1 processes files locally in the browser.

What works today?

Merge, split, rotate, reorder, delete, preview, and export for PDF documents.

Why are some tools missing?

Browser-only launch v1 is intentionally focused on the workflows we can make reliable and polished right now.

How do I report an issue?

Open a GitHub issue or use the support contact listed in the repo.
