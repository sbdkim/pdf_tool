Privacy

Browser-only processing

Organize PDF Pages launch v1 processes files locally in the browser. Documents are not intentionally uploaded to a remote server.

Telemetry

If lightweight analytics are enabled, they should measure product usage without collecting document content.

Support data

If you contact support, only the information you choose to share in your issue or message will be available.
